# KLARO — Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0

# Brand Overview

## Name

Klaro

---

## Meaning

Inspired by the Tagalog word:

"Klaro"

Meaning:

Clear  
Understood  
Makes sense

Additional resonance:

German:
"Klar"

Meaning:

Clear

---

## Tagline

Confidence through clarity.

(Status: Working version)

---

## Brand Promise

Klaro helps people become comfortable using AI through guided everyday tasks.

Not by teaching technology.

By making it clearer.

---

# Brand Positioning

For people who feel overwhelmed by AI,

Klaro provides guided everyday experiences that transform uncertainty into confidence through structured support, transparent assistance, and reflection.
